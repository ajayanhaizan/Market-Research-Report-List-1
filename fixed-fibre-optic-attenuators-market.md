<p><strong>The Fixed Fibre Optic Attenuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulkhead/Plug/Panel Mount</li><li>In-Line Jumper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed fibre optic attenuators market comprises three main types: bulkhead, plug, and panel mount, as well as in-line jumper attenuators. Bulkhead attenuators are designed for integration into panels, enabling easy connection and disconnection. Plug attenuators are used for direct coupling to connectors, providing flexibility in various setups. Panel mount attenuators are installed on racks or enclosures for permanent installations. In-line jumper attenuators are integrated into patch cables, allowing for adjustments in signal strength without reconfiguration of existing installations.</p></p>
