<p><p>The air heaters market includes several types, each designed for specific applications. Indirect fired air heaters use a heat exchanger to warm air without direct combustion contact, ensuring cleaner air. Direct fired air heaters burn fuel directly, providing instant heat. Duct heaters are installed within air distribution systems, efficiently warming air before it enters living spaces. Portable air heaters offer flexibility and mobility for localized heating. Other market types may include electric heaters and infrared heaters, catering to diverse heating needs.</p></p>
