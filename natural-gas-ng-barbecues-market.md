<p><strong>The Natural Gas (NG) Barbecues Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Device</li><li>Embedded Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural Gas (NG) barbecues can be categorized into two market types: self-contained devices and embedded devices. Self-contained devices are standalone units that include built-in fuel storage and ignition systems, making them portable and convenient for outdoor use. In contrast, embedded devices are integrated into outdoor kitchens or fixed installations, relying on a direct gas line for fuel supply, providing a permanent and often more sophisticated barbecuing solution. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for convenience and permanence in outdoor cooking.</p></p>
