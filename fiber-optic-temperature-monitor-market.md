<p><ul><li>Distributed</li><li>Non-distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic temperature monitor market can be categorized into two primary types: distributed and non-distributed systems. Distributed temperature monitoring involves using fiber optic cables to measure temperature along their entire length, enabling real-time monitoring over extensive areas. This type is ideal for applications like pipelines and tunnels. In contrast, non-distributed systems focus on point measurements at specific locations, offering high precision for localized temperature monitoring, suitable for applications like data centers and industrial equipment.</p></p>
