<p><p>The slurry valves market comprises two primary types: wafer-type and full-flanged type valves. Wafer-type slurry valves are compact, lightweight, and designed to fit between flanges, offering ease of installation and maintenance, making them suitable for tight spaces. In contrast, full-flanged type valves have flanges on both ends, providing a robust connection and superior sealing, ideal for high-pressure and abrasive applications. Each valve type is tailored for specific industrial needs, enhancing operational efficiency in slurry handling.</p></p>
