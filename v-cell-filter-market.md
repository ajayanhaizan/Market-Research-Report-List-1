<p><p>The V-Cell filter market encompasses various types, primarily classified into 2V-type, 3V-type, and 4V-type filters. The 2V-type filter features two vertical cell layers, providing moderate filtration efficiency. In contrast, the 3V-type filter incorporates three layers, enhancing performance through improved airflow and filtration capabilities. The 4V-type filter includes four layers, offering the highest efficiency and longest service life, making it suitable for demanding applications. Together, these filters cater to diverse industrial needs for particulate and microbial control.</p></p>
