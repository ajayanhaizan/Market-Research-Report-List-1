<p><p>The Oil and Gas Water Purification System market comprises various technologies to treat wastewater. Membrane processes utilize filters to separate contaminants, offering high efficiency. Thermal treatment involves boiling water to evaporate impurities, followed by condensation. Ion exchange uses resin beads to replace unwanted ions with desirable ones, effectively removing pollutants. Capacitive deionization works by applying an electrical field to adsorb ions onto electrodes, enabling efficient water purification. Each method caters to specific needs in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
