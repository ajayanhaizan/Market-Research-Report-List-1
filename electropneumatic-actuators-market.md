<p><p>Electropneumatic actuators are devices that use electrical signals to control pneumatic systems. The market for these actuators is categorized into two main types: single acting and double acting. Single acting actuators utilize air pressure to move in one direction and rely on a spring for return, making them simpler and often more compact. In contrast, double acting actuators employ air pressure for both extension and retraction, offering more power and precise control, thus catering to more demanding applications in various industries.</p></p>
