<p><p>The Intelligent Fire Detection System market consists of two primary types: Addressable Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems offer advanced capabilities, allowing individual detectors to be monitored and identified on a central panel, enhancing response and management efficiency. In contrast, Conventional Fire Detection Systems operate with grouped detectors, identifying a general area of concern but lacking precise location tracking. Both types cater to varying needs, with addressable systems preferred for complex environments requiring detailed monitoring.</p></p>
