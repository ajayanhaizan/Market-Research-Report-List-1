<p><strong>The Escalator Traction Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gearless Traction Machine</li><li>Geared Traction Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The escalator traction machine market consists of two primary types: gearless and geared traction machines. Gearless traction machines utilize a direct drive system, resulting in higher efficiency, reduced maintenance, and lower energy consumption. They are often preferred for high-rise applications due to their compact design and quiet operation. Geared traction machines, on the other hand, use a gearing system to transmit power, making them suitable for lower-height buildings and providing more torque at lower speeds. Each type caters to specific operational needs and performance requirements in escalator systems.</p></p>
