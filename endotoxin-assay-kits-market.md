<p><p>Endotoxin assay kits are essential for detecting bacterial endotoxins in pharmaceuticals and medical devices. The market includes three main types: Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, which uses gel formation as an indicator of endotoxin presence; Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, utilizing colorimetric changes for sensitivity and quantification; and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test, measuring the increase in turbidity caused by endotoxin-liposome interactions. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to various industry requirements for safety and compliance.</p></p>
