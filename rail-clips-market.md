<p><p>The rail clips market primarily includes two types: weldable clips and boltable clips. Weldable clips are attached directly to the rail, providing a rigid connection that enhances stability and reduces vibration. They are often used in heavy-duty applications where durability is crucial. Boltable clips, on the other hand, use bolts for attachment, allowing for easier installation and maintenance, and are ideal for applications that require flexibility in rail positioning. Each type serves distinct operational needs within the rail industry.</p></p>
