<p><ul><li>Continuous Flight Augers</li><li>Bucket Augers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auger Drilling Equipment market comprises various types, notably Continuous Flight Augers and Bucket Augers. Continuous Flight Augers feature a helical screw design, allowing for efficient soil and material removal during drilling, ideal for deep applications. In contrast, Bucket Augers incorporate a bucket mechanism to capture and lift loose material, making them suitable for shallow drilling and sampling tasks. Both types are essential for construction, geotechnical investigations, and environmental assessments, offering unique advantages depending on project requirements.</p></p>
