<p><p>The synthetic polymer waterproofing membrane market features two primary types: sheet-based membranes and liquid-applied membranes. Sheet-based membranes are prefabricated materials that provide strong barriers against water infiltration, often used in roofs, basements, and foundations. Liquid-applied membranes, on the other hand, are versatile coatings that are applied directly to surfaces, forming a seamless waterproof layer. Both types offer effective protection against water damage and are utilized in various construction and infrastructure projects, enhancing durability and longevity.</p></p>
