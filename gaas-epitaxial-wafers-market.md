<p><ul><li>LEC Grown GaAs</li><li>VGF Grown GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaAs epitaxial wafers market encompasses various production methods, notably LEC (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) and VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown GaAs. LEC grown GaAs wafers are produced using a liquid encapsulation process that improves crystal quality and reduces defects, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Conversely, VGF grown GaAs wafers utilize a vertical crystallization technique, offering superior uniformity and scalability. Both types serve crucial roles in electronics, photonics, and optoelectronic devices, catering to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
