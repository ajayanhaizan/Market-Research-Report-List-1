<p><strong>The Fabric Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seals in the Fibers</li><li>Penetrates the Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fabric protection market types include "Seals in the Fibers" and "Penetrates the Fiber." Seals in the Fibers refers to protective treatments that form a barrier on the fabric's surface, repelling liquids and stains without altering the material's texture. Penetrates the Fiber involves treatments that absorb into the fabric, bonding at a molecular level to provide long-lasting protection while maintaining breathability. Both methods aim to enhance durability, resist stains, and prolong fabric life, catering to different material needs and user preferences.</p></p>
