<p><strong>The Decontamination Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Based Methods</li><li>Chemicals Based Methods</li><li>Biology Based Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decontamination equipment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Physical Based Methods** involve techniques such as heat, filtration, and radiation to eliminate contaminants without the use of chemicals. </p><p>2. **Chemicals Based Methods** utilize disinfectants, solvents, and sterilants to chemically neutralize or remove harmful agents. </p><p>3. **Biology Based Methods** leverage biological processes, such as the use of enzymes or microorganisms, to degrade or render contaminants harmless, focusing on eco-friendly solutions. Each method addresses specific contamination challenges across various industries.</p></p>
