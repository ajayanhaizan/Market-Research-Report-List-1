<p><p>The jet mill market consists of several types, including fluidized bed jet mills and spiral jet mills. Fluidized bed jet mills utilize a high-speed gas stream to achieve fine particle size through the suspension of materials in a fluidized bed, ensuring uniformity and reducing thermal degradation. Spiral jet mills, on the other hand, utilize spiraled air jets to create high shear and particle collision, optimizing particle size distribution. Other jet mills encompass various designs, catering to specific applications and material properties.</p></p>
