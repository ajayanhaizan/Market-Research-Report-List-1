<p><p>Marine alarm systems can be categorized into two main types: Remote Type and Connected Type. Remote Type systems operate independently, utilizing onboard alarms that alert crew members to potential hazards. These systems typically rely on manual checks and alerts. In contrast, Connected Type systems integrate with broader networks, enabling real-time monitoring and alerts to be sent to mobile devices or centralized management systems. This connectivity enhances response times and situational awareness, significantly improving safety and operational efficiency in marine environments.</p></p>
