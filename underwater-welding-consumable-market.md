Electrodes are used for producing strong welds, while rods serve as filler material during the welding process. Wires provide electrical conduction and are often used in submerged arc welding. Fluxes aid in shielding the weld from contaminants and enhance bonding. Other consumables may include specialized coatings and tools aimed at improving efficiency and quality. Each type plays a crucial role in safe and effective underwater welding operations.</p></p>
