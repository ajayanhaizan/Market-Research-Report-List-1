<p><p>The disinfectant cleaner market comprises several types based on active ingredients. Chlorine disinfectants use sodium hypochlorite to kill pathogens effectively. Peroxide disinfectants, utilizing hydrogen peroxide, offer strong antibacterial properties. Aldehyde disinfectants, such as formaldehyde, are potent but require careful handling. Alcohol disinfectants, primarily ethanol and isopropyl alcohol, provide rapid action against germs. Iodine-containing disinfectants are effective for skin antisepsis. Phenol disinfectants, known for their antimicrobial properties, are often used in healthcare settings. The "Other" category includes various specialized formulations.</p></p>
