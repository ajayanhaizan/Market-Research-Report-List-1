<p><strong>The Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phaneromer Quartz</li><li>Cryptocrystalline Quartz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered quartz (E-Quartz) is a composite material made primarily from natural quartz crystals combined with resins and pigments. It is categorized into two main types: phaneromer quartz, consisting of visible crystalline structures that provide a more natural appearance, and cryptocrystalline quartz, where the crystals are too small to be seen with the naked eye, resulting in a uniform and smooth finish. Both types are popular for countertops and surfaces due to their durability, beauty, and low maintenance requirements.</p></p>
