<p><strong>The Coin Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coin battery holder market is primarily categorized into two types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount holders are designed to attach directly to the surface of a circuit board, providing a compact design and ease of automation during assembly. In contrast, through hole holders require a hole drilled in the circuit board, leading to better mechanical stability and reliable connections. Both types cater to various applications, including consumer electronics, medical devices, and industrial equipment, based on design preferences and spatial constraints.</p></p>
