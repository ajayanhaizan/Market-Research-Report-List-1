<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Differential Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Differential Relay</li><li>Biased or Percentage Differential Relay</li><li>Voltage Balance Differential Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The differential protection relay market can be segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Current Differential Relay**: Monitors the difference in current between two circuits, ensuring protection against faults by tripping when a significant imbalance is detected.</p><p>2. **Biased or Percentage Differential Relay**: Incorporates a bias feature to account for load fluctuations, functioning effectively under normal conditions while providing fault protection.</p><p>3. **Voltage Balance Differential Relay**: Operates on voltage levels across phases, detecting imbalances that indicate faults, thus enhancing protection for transformers and generators.</p></p>
