<p><p>The electrical insulating rubber gloves market is categorized into two main types: low voltage and high voltage gloves. Low voltage electrical insulating rubber gloves are designed for use in environments where the voltage does not exceed 1,000 volts, providing protection during tasks like troubleshooting and maintenance. In contrast, high voltage electrical insulating rubber gloves are intended for situations involving voltages above 1,000 volts, offering enhanced protection for utility workers and electricians dealing with high-voltage equipment. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and preventing electrical hazards.</p></p>
