<p><p>Gravity table market involves various separation techniques based on the principle of density differences. Multi-gravity separation uses enhanced centrifugal forces to separate materials, offering higher efficiency for fine particle recovery. Jigging separation relies on pulsed water movement to segregate materials by density, making it effective for coarser minerals. Shaking tables utilize a combination of gravity and shaking motion to allow particles to stratify according to weight and size, enabling precise separation in mineral processing and recycling applications.</p></p>
