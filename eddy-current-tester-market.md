<p><p>The Eddy Current Tester market is primarily divided into two types: contact testers and non-contact testers. Contact testers require direct physical contact with the test material to detect flaws and measure conductivity, making them suitable for precise evaluations on accessible surfaces. In contrast, non-contact testers use electromagnetic fields to assess defects without requiring physical contact, allowing for testing on delicate materials or at high speeds. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring reliable quality control in sectors like aerospace and manufacturing.</p></p>
