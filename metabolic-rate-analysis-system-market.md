<p><p>The Metabolic Rate Analysis System market comprises two primary segments: VO2 Max Analysis Systems and RMR (Resting Metabolic Rate) Analysis Systems. VO2 Max systems measure the maximum oxygen consumption during intense exercise, assessing aerobic fitness and endurance performance. In contrast, RMR systems evaluate the amount of energy expended at rest, providing insights into metabolism and caloric needs. Both systems are essential tools in sports science, healthcare, and fitness industries, aiding in optimizing training regimens and weight management strategies.</p></p>
