<p><p>The activated carbon market encompasses various types, each tailored for specific applications. Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) is finely ground for superior adsorption in liquid phase treatments. Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) features larger particles, ideal for gas phase filtration and water treatment. Extruded Activated Carbon has uniform cylindrical shapes, enhancing mechanical strength and flow rates. Bead Activated Carbon offers spherical shapes for specialized applications. Other types may include composite materials and tailor-made forms for niche applications, addressing diverse environmental and industrial needs.</p></p>
