<p><p>The solar-powered greenhouse fan market can be divided primarily into two types: circular fans and exhaust fans. Circular fans are designed for airflow distribution within the greenhouse, promoting even temperature and humidity levels, which is vital for optimal plant growth. In contrast, exhaust fans facilitate the removal of hot air and excess humidity, ensuring a healthy environment. Both fan types leverage solar energy, making them eco-friendly options that reduce dependency on electricity while enhancing greenhouse efficiency.</p></p>
