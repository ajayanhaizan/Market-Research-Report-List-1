<p><p>The disinfecting medical equipment market comprises three main types: steam disinfecting, dry heat disinfecting, and chemical disinfecting. </p><p>Steam disinfecting uses high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making it effective for heat-resistant items. Dry heat disinfecting involves elevated temperatures without moisture, suitable for items sensitive to steam. Chemical disinfecting employs liquid or gas agents to destroy pathogens, offering versatility for diverse materials. Each method serves distinct applications, addressing varying infection control needs in healthcare settings while ensuring equipment safety and efficacy.</p></p>
