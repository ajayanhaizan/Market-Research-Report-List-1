<p><strong>The Glass Cutting Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Water Soluble Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass cutting oil market is primarily categorized into two types: mineral oil and water-soluble oil. Mineral oil, derived from petroleum, offers excellent lubrication and protects glass against chipping during cutting processes. It is favored for its durability and effectiveness in high-speed cutting. On the other hand, water-soluble oil is mixed with water to reduce viscosity and enhance cooling, making it environmentally friendly and easier to clean. Both types serve crucial roles in optimizing glass cutting efficiency and precision.</p></p>
