<p><p>The High Voltage Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCC) market is categorized into two main types: chip type and lead type. Chip type capacitors are compact, surface-mounted devices widely used in electronics due to their space-saving attributes and efficiency in high-frequency applications. In contrast, lead type capacitors feature extended leads for through-hole mounting, offering enhanced robustness and better connection stability, making them suitable for high-power applications. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
