<p><strong>The Drone Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADS-B Compatible</li><li>ADS-B Non-Compatible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drone transponders market is categorized into ADS-B Compatible and ADS-B Non-Compatible types. ADS-B Compatible transponders communicate with air traffic controllers and other aircraft, enhancing situational awareness and safety in shared airspace. These devices adhere to the Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast (ADS-B) system, facilitating real-time data sharing. In contrast, ADS-B Non-Compatible transponders do not support this communication, limiting their integration with air traffic management systems. This distinction influences regulatory compliance, usage scenarios, and market adoption among drone operators.</p></p>
