<p><p>The commodities sweeteners ingredients market encompasses various low-calorie and artificial sweeteners, each with unique properties and applications. Sucralose offers a sugar-like taste without calories, making it popular in beverages. Aspartame is widely used in low-sugar products but is more sensitive to heat. Stevia, derived from a plant, is favored for its natural origin. Saccharin is one of the oldest sweeteners, often used in processed foods. Neotame is a potent, stable sweetener ideal for food applications requiring high sweetness without calories.</p></p>
