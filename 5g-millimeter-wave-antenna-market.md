<p><p>The 5G millimeter wave antenna market includes two primary types: Antenna on Chip (AoC) and Antenna in Package (AiP). AoC integrates antennas directly onto semiconductor chips, optimizing space and performance for compact devices. In contrast, AiP involves packaging antennas with other components, enhancing efficiency and reducing signal loss. Both types are essential for enabling high-speed, low-latency 5G communication, catering to diverse applications such as smartphones, IoT devices, and advanced telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
