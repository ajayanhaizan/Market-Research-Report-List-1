<p><p>Addison Disease testing comprises mainly two market types: laboratory testing and imaging studies. Imaging studies, such as CT or MRI scans, are used to identify adrenal gland abnormalities, providing insight into the structural causes of adrenal insufficiency. Together, these testing modalities facilitate accurate diagnosis and effective treatment strategies for Addison Disease.</p></p>
