<p><p>The Corrugated Aluminum Sheath Cable market is primarily divided into two types: HV (High Voltage) and EHV (Extra High Voltage) Corrugated Aluminum Sheath Cables. HV cables are designed for voltage levels ranging from 35 kV to 230 kV, suited for power transmission and distribution. EHV cables, on the other hand, operate above 230 kV, catering to long-distance transmission needs while minimizing losses. Both types utilize a corrugated aluminum sheath to enhance mechanical strength and protect against environmental factors, ensuring reliable performance.</p></p>
