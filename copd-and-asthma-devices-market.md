<p><p>The COPD and asthma devices market comprises various inhalation delivery systems. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) deliver a fixed dose of medication in aerosol form. Drug Powder Inhalers (DPIs) utilize dry powder for inhalation, requiring patient effort for activation. Soft Mist Inhalers create a slow-moving therapeutic mist for easier inhalation. Jet Nebulizers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol, while Ultrasonic Nebulizers employ high-frequency vibrations. Other devices include spacer devices and breath-actuated inhalers, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
