AlN chucks offer superior thermal conductivity and electrical insulation, ideal for high-performance environments. SiC chucks are recognized for their exceptional hardness and thermal properties, enhancing durability and performance in demanding conditions. Each type serves unique requirements in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and precision machining.</p></p>
