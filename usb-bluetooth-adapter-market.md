<p><ul><li>Single-band Bluetooth Adapter</li><li>Dual-band Bluetooth Adapter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB Bluetooth adapter market is categorized into single-band and dual-band adapters. Single-band Bluetooth adapters operate on the 2.4 GHz frequency, providing basic connectivity for devices like keyboards and mice, making them cost-effective for simple tasks. In contrast, dual-band Bluetooth adapters utilize both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering enhanced data transfer speeds and reduced interference, which is ideal for high-performance applications such as audio streaming and gaming. This differentiation caters to varying consumer needs and device compatibility.</p></p>
