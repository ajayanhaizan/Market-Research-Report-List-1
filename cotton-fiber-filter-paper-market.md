<p><ul><li>Qualitative Filter Paper</li><li>Quantitative Filter Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton fiber filter paper market is divided into two main types: qualitative and quantitative filter paper. Qualitative filter paper is designed for general filtration purposes, allowing users to distinguish between various physical properties of substances, such as clarity and color. In contrast, quantitative filter paper is engineered for accurate measurements, often used in gravimetric analysis where precise weight measurements are crucial. Both types serve critical roles in laboratory settings, offering distinct advantages depending on the application and desired outcomes.</p></p>
