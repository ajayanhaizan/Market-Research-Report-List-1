<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real Time PCR kits market is primarily divided into two types: One-step Real Time PCR and Two-step Real Time PCR. One-step Real Time PCR combines the reverse transcription and amplification processes into a single reaction, streamlining procedures and reducing contamination risks. In contrast, Two-step Real Time PCR separates these steps, allowing for greater flexibility and control over each stage, which is beneficial for complex analyses. Both methods cater to diverse research and diagnostic applications, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
