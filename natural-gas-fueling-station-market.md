<p><p>The Natural Gas Fuelling Station market is primarily divided into two segments: CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) fueling stations. CNG fueling stations compress natural gas to fill high-pressure tanks for vehicles, commonly used for light-duty vehicles and public transit. In contrast, LNG fueling stations cool natural gas to liquid form, allowing for more efficient storage and transportation, ideal for heavy-duty vehicles and long-haul applications. Both segments support the transition to cleaner energy sources in the transportation sector.</p></p>
