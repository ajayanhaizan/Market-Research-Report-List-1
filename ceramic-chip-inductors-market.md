<p><ul><li>Multi-layer Ceramic Chip Inductor</li><li>Wire Wound Ceramic Chip Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic chip inductors market is primarily categorized into two types: multi-layer ceramic chip inductors and wire wound ceramic chip inductors. Multi-layer ceramic chip inductors feature multiple layers of ceramic material, providing a compact design with enhanced inductance values, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. In contrast, wire wound ceramic chip inductors utilize a wire coil wound around a ceramic core, offering higher performance at lower frequencies while maintaining robustness. Both types cater to diverse electronics applications, ensuring efficient power management and signal filtering.</p></p>
