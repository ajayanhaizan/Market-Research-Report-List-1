<p><p>Automatic Platform Screen Doors (APSDs) are modern safety installations found at the edges of train and metro platforms. They act as physical barriers between the platform and the tracks, enhancing passenger safety by preventing accidents, reducing the risk of unauthorized track access, and maintaining climate control within stations. Their design integrates with train operations, opening only when a train is properly aligned at the platform.</p><p>The Automatic Platform Screen Door Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Automatic Platform Screen Door (APSD) market includes three main types: Full-Closed Type, Semi-Closed Type, and Half Height Type. Full-Closed Type doors provide complete separation between passengers and the tracks, enhancing safety and climate control. Semi-Closed Type offers partial barriers, balancing safety with accessibility. Half Height Type is shorter, allowing for ventilation and ease of movement while still providing a deterrent against accidents. Each type caters to different transit needs and passenger experiences in urban rail systems.</p></p>
