Pneumatic forklift tires feature air-filled constructions, providing better shock absorption and traction, ideal for outdoor use. Solid forklift tires, made from resilient rubber, offer durability and are suitable for indoor operations, minimizing downtime due to punctures. Polyurethane forklift tires combine lightweight properties with excellent wear resistance, making them perfect for high-traffic areas and providing smooth rides while protecting floor surfaces.</p></p>
