<p><p>Radiant Energy Welding encompasses advanced techniques like Laser Beam Welding and Electron Beam Welding (EBW). Laser Beam Welding utilizes concentrated light energy to fuse materials, offering precision and minimal thermal distortion, making it ideal for thin components and intricate designs. In contrast, Electron Beam Welding employs a focused beam of electrons in a vacuum, allowing for deep penetration and high-speed welding, particularly suited for thick materials. Both methods are employed across industries for their efficiency, accuracy, and ability to join dissimilar materials.</p></p>
