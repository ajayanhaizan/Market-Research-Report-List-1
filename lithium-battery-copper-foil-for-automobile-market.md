<p><p>The lithium battery copper foil market for automobiles is primarily divided into two types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electrolytic process, offering high conductivity and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-performance batteries. In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by rolling copper sheets into thin foils, providing enhanced mechanical properties and flexibility. Both types play crucial roles in battery efficiency and overall vehicle performance, essential for the advancement of electric vehicles.</p></p>
